About:

This is a visuzaliztion of a vanilla feedforward nueral network that classifies handwritten digits. The four white boxes on top illustrate the process of normalizing the images. The pixel values of the normalzed image on the right are fed directly as input neurons into the neural network.

Neurons are organized into layers and drawn vertically as thin lines or rectangles, and synapses as connecting lines using these colors:

  • Cyan neuron = strong activation
  • Black neuron = weak or zero activation
  • Cyan synapse =
  • Black neuron = weak or zero activation
